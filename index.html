À PROPOS

Jules Bouchier-Végis works as a freelance designer since 2010 between Brussels and Marseille. He accompanies industrial actors and artistic structures on innovative programs. His role covers the fields of consulting to identity, through design, prototyping, production monitoring and distribution. Its missions are articulated around a global approach that concerns the area of object design and space planning. Since 2023, it has opened up to an authorial practice whose ambition is to extract new realities through the exploration of an experimental vocabulary of forms.

Jules Bouchier-Végis travaille comme designer indépendant depuis 2010 entre Bruxelles et Marseille.
